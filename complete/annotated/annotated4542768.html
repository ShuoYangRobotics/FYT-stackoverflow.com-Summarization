Try random.sample : 

>>> sides = 6
>>> dice = random.sample(range(1, sides + 1), 3)

I 'd advise that you reconsider whether it is a good idea to have variables called die1 , die2 , die3 .

It is usually better to use a list as in the above example .

import random,time
N=80000
a = range(1,7)

for i in xrange(N):
    random.shuffle(a)
    a[:3]
