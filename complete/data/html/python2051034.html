Question (ID-2051034):

why is this an infinite loop in python?

I can't seem to figure out why this is an infinite loop in python??

for i in range(n):
    j=1
    while((i*j)<n):
       j+=1

shouldn't the outer loop go n times. incrementing j until its equal to n div i each time?
