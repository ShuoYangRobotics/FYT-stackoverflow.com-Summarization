2. Every second in a-n: a c e g i k m

>>> import string
>>> string.lowercase[:14]
'abcdefghijklmn'
>>> string.lowercase[:14:2]
'acegikm'

To do the urls, you could use something like this

[i+j for i,j in zip(list_of_urls, string.lowercase[:14])]
